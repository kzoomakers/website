How this makerspace works

  • This is a 501(c)(3) non-profit, nobody is paid, no profit is made.
  • Everyone volunteers, from donating equipment to sweeping the floors. Without volunteers we cannot survive.
  • Companies and individuals can donate equipment to make our space a benefit to the Kalamazoo Area.
  • We all pay a monthly membership to cover costs like rent, electric, consumables, repairs, etc.
  • Membership costs are tiered based on insurance costs and equipment costs.

What We Offer

We are a mix between a club and a library â€” rent access to tools and equipment, and gain entry to a creative community. Hang out with others who design, build, and explore new skills across metalworking, woodworking, lasercutting, 3D printing, electronics, crafts, and more.

Wood Shop

Members have donated some exceptional woodworking equipment. Our complete shop boasts a range of tools and machinery that cater to both beginners and seasoned artisans. Whether you're embarking on your first project or looking to elevate your craft, we have everything you need to bring your vision to life.

Industrial Lasers

We have state-of-the-art laser technology, designed to transform your projects into works of art. Our Class IV lasers, boasting up to 150 watts of power, are ready to take your craftsmanship to new heights by offering both wood and acrylic engraving and cutting capabilities.

3D Printing

Our 3D printing hub is your gateway to turning your ideas into tangible reality. We take pride in offering a range of cutting-edge 3D printers along with an extensive selection of filament materials, enabling you to bring your concept to life, one layer at a time.

Electronics Workbench

From soldering irons to oscilloscopes, bench power supplies, and more, we've got you covered. Plus, if you're eager to delve into the world of Arduino or Raspberry Pi, look no further â€“ this is your go-to place!

Craft Room

Our craft room is where dreams come to life, and imagination knows no bounds. Whether you're into fabric arts, jewelry making, or any other craft, our well-equipped space is designed to handle it all.
