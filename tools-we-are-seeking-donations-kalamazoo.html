Support Our Kalamazoo Nonprofit with Your Donation

Your donation to our Kalamazoo nonprofit makerspace empowers the community. When you donate tools and equipment to our nonprofit organization, you're investing in education, innovation, and opportunity for all of Southwest Michigan.

Why Donate to Kalamazoo Makerspace Nonprofit?

As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in Kalamazoo, we rely on generous donations from our community. Your nonprofit donation helps us:

  • Serve the Kalamazoo Community: Your donation supports free workshops, educational programs, and community access to tools
  • Tax-Deductible Giving: All donations to our Kalamazoo nonprofit are tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law
  • Build Local Capacity: Donate equipment that empowers makers, entrepreneurs, and students throughout Southwest Michigan
  • Create Lasting Impact: One donated tool can enable hundreds of projects and skill-building opportunities

How to Donate to Our Nonprofit in Kalamazoo

Donating to our Kalamazoo nonprofit makerspace is easy! We accept tool donations, equipment donations, and supply donations from individuals and businesses throughout Michigan. Whether you're in Kalamazoo, Battle Creek, Portage, or anywhere in Southwest Michigan, your nonprofit donation makes a difference.

Contact us today to arrange your tax-deductible donation!
