How our makerspace works

We're a combination of a club and a library: a place where you don’t borrow books, but access to equipment and the community.

We’re a 501(c)(3) Domestic Nonprofit Corporation, run off donated equipment and volunteer support. We don't have million dollar endowments. Every dollar goes into keeping the lights on and the tools spinning.

What makes this place extraordinary isn’t infrastructure — it’s people. Everything runs on volunteer power: the tours, the cleaning, the restocking, the mentoring. Even our board and president work shoulder to shoulder with everyone else.

We seek any who want to make or build and share their experiments or ideas.

Sneak Peak Gallery

See how we make ideas happen. From late-night solder sessions to spontaneous art-meets-engineering builds, our makerspace is a playground for curiosity. No two days look the same—3D printers hum beside laser cutters, paint splatters near code on whiteboards, and laughter blends with the sound of power tools. This gallery isn’t just photos—it’s a glimpse of what it feels like to create, collaborate, and occasionally break things (intentionally). Step inside our world of prototypes, passion projects, and pure creative chaos.

Meet Our Board of Directors
