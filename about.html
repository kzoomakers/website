How this makerspace works

We're a combination of a club and a library: a place where you don’t borrow books, but access to equipment and the community.

We’re a 501(c)(3) Domestic Nonprofit Corporation, run off donated equipment and volunteer support. We don't have million dollar endowments. Every dollar goes into keeping the lights on and the tools spinning.

What makes this place extraordinary isn’t infrastructure — it’s people. Everything runs on volunteer power: the tours, the cleaning, the restocking, the mentoring. Even our board and president work shoulder to shoulder with everyone else.

We seek any who want to make or build and share their experiments or ideas.

Our Mission

To be a community that fosters creativity and innovation by providing a collaborative workspace equipped with tools and resources. We inspire individuals to design, learn, and explore new skills in a hands-on environment. By uniting local artists, crafters, engineers, makers, and thinkers, to nurture the transformation of ideas into tangible creations.

Vision

We envision a future where imagination drives progress and every person feels empowered to turn curiosity into creation. Kalamazoo will be known as a place where ideas spark connection, innovation shapes possibility, and creativity becomes a shared force for community growth.
