How our makerspace works

We're a combination of a club and a library: a place where you don’t borrow books, but access to equipment and the community.

We’re a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, sustained not by billion-dollar endowments, but by donated equipment and member support. Every dollar goes into keeping the lights on and the tools turning.

What makes this place extraordinary isn’t infrastructure — it’s people. Everything runs on volunteer power: the tours, the cleaning, the restocking, the mentoring. Even our board and CEO work shoulder to shoulder with everyone else.

We want people who want to make things and share those ideas and creations.

Our Mission

To offer a collaborative workspace with equipment and programs. Hands-on environment for creativity, exploration, and building new skills. A place our community can turn their ideas into creations by fostering a culture of local innovation.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual with an idea, no matter their background, has equal access to tools, community, and support to invent, build and share. We succeed when our space fuels both first-time creators and seasoned makers to solve real problems, tell new stories, and build their own paths forward — together.
