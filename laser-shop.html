Supported Materials

Materials You CAN Laser Cut/Engrave:

  • Wood (up to 2" thick)
  • Plywood and MDF
  • Acrylic and Plexiglass
  • Leather and Fabric
  • Paper and Cardboard
  • Cork and Rubber
  • Anodized Aluminum (engraving only)
  • Glass (engraving only)

Materials You CANNOT Laser:

  • ❌ PVC or Vinyl (releases toxic chlorine gas)
  • ❌ ABS Plastic (releases toxic fumes)
  • ❌ Polycarbonate (discolors and releases fumes)
  • ❌ Fiberglass (releases harmful particles)
  • ❌ Coated Carbon Fiber

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my own materials?

Yes, members provide their own materials for laser cutting projects. We can help you source appropriate materials and recommend suppliers.

Which laser cutting and engraving software should I use - Ruby or Lightburn?

Both are excellent options! Ruby is the native Trotec software with advanced features, while Lightburn is popular for its user-friendly interface. Our trainers can help you with either one for your laser cutting and engraving projects.

How thick of material can I laser cut or engrave?

Our Trotec lasers can laser cut wood up to 2 inches thick. Cutting depth varies by material type and density, and laser engraving can be done on even thicker materials.

How do I get trained on the laser cutters and engravers?

After becoming a member, you can schedule training with one of our multiple qualified trainers. Training covers laser safety, laser cutting and engraving software operation, and material handling.

Can I engrave photos or complex designs with the laser cutter?

Absolutely! Our lasers excel at complex laser engravings including photographs, detailed artwork, and intricate patterns. We'll teach you how to prepare your files for optimal laser cutting and engraving results.

What file formats do the laser cutters accept for cutting and engraving?

Both Ruby and Lightburn support common vector formats (SVG, AI, DXF) and raster formats (JPG, PNG, BMP) for laser cutting and engraving. We'll help you prepare your designs properly for the laser.
