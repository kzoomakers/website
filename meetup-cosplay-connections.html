Welcome to Kalamazoo's Cosplay Community

Check Calendar for Meetup Times

Crafting Cosplay Connections is a community-driven cosplay group based in Kalamazoo, Michigan, hosted by Kzoo Makers. We bring together cosplayers, costume makers, prop builders, artists, and fandom enthusiasts of all skill levels to connect, share knowledge, and support one another in a welcoming, inclusive environment.

What We Do

Our meetings are discussion-based and social, typically centered around a specific cosplay-related topic while also leaving time for open discussion and sharing. We gather to:

  • Talk Through Current Projects - Share what you're working on and get feedback
  • Plan Future Cosplays - Brainstorm ideas and get inspired by others
  • Share Creative Ideas - Exchange techniques, materials, and approaches
  • Troubleshoot Challenges - Get advice and multiple possible solutions
  • Learn From Shared Experiences - Benefit from the community's collective knowledge
  • Connect With Fellow Fans - Meet others who share your fandoms and interests

Our Philosophy: Cosplay Is For Everyone

At Crafting Cosplay Connections, we believe cosplay is for everyone—regardless of:

  • Experience Level - From first-timers to competition veterans
  • Budget - Thrift store finds to premium materials
  • Body Type - Every body is a cosplay body
  • Fandom - Anime, gaming, comics, movies, original characters, and more

We value collaboration, encouragement, and learning from one another over perfection. There's no judgment here—just a supportive community helping each other grow.

Building Kalamazoo's Cosplay Community

The goal of Crafting Cosplay Connections is to build a strong local cosplay community in Kalamazoo and West Michigan. We aim to:

  • Support both beginner and experienced cosplayers
  • Create a consistent, low-pressure space where people can ask questions
  • Provide a place to share progress and celebrate achievements
  • Help cosplayers grow more confident in their creative journey

Whether you're planning your first cosplay or your fiftieth, you'll find a welcoming community ready to help you succeed.

Frequently Asked Questions

When and where does Crafting Cosplay Connections meet?

We meet at Kzoo Makers in Kalamazoo, Michigan. Check our calendar for current meeting times and dates.

Check Calendar for Times

Do I need to be a Kzoo Makers member to attend?

No! Participation in Crafting Cosplay Connections does not require makerspace membership. Our meetings focus on community connection rather than use of tools or equipment. This makes our group an accessible entry point for anyone interested in cosplay.

I've never cosplayed before. Can I still come?

Absolutely! We welcome complete beginners. Our community believes cosplay is for everyone, and we're happy to help newcomers get started on their cosplay journey. You don't need to have a project in progress—just come ready to learn and connect!

Do I need to bring anything to the meetup?

Nope! Our meetings are discussion-based and social. You're welcome to bring works-in-progress to share or get feedback on, but it's not required. Just bring yourself and your enthusiasm for cosplay!

What fandoms are represented in the group?

All of them! Our members cosplay from anime, video games, comics, movies, TV shows, books, and even original characters. Whatever you're into, you're welcome here.

Is this group only for people who make their own costumes?

Not at all! Whether you make everything from scratch, modify purchased items, commission pieces, or do closet cosplay, you're welcome. We celebrate all approaches to cosplay.

What if I want to use the makerspace tools for my cosplay?

If you're interested in using Kzoo Makers' tools and equipment (sewing machines, 3D printers, laser cutters, etc.) for your cosplay projects, you can explore membership options. But this is completely optional—you can participate in our meetups without being a member!
