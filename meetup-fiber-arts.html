What Makes Us Special

Fiber art can be anything from a crocheted coaster to an impressive cosplay creation and is limited only by the imagination.

The strength of fiber arts at Kzoo Makers is not simply the machines and tools available, it's the community that makes it special.

Each week our community of fiber artists comes together to create, embellish, and evolve fibers and textiles into art. Knowledge is shared among the group, with expertise in:

  • Knitting
  • Loom Knitting
  • Crochet
  • Embroidery
  • Cross Stitch
  • Felting
  • Sewing
  • And so much more!

Learn and Grow Together

The fiber arts community has hosted several workshops, including Sewing Machines 101 where the basics of using a sewing machine are covered, as well as a food safe yarn dyeing workshop. Our experienced members are always willing to share their knowledge and help newcomers get started.

If you've ever been amazed by the wizardry of making fabric using a string and a hook (or two sticks), been enthralled by the beauty of complex colorful cross stitch, or wanted to learn to make your own clothing, the fiber arts community at Kzoo Makers has something for you.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced fiber artist, you'll find a welcoming community ready to share techniques, troubleshoot problems, and celebrate your creations. Bring your current project or start something new with guidance from our talented members.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the fiber arts meetup happen?

Every Saturday at 4:00 PM! Drop in anytime during the meetup to work on your projects and connect with fellow fiber artists.

Do I need to be a member to attend?

No, but if you want to use any tools owned by us like sewing machines etc, you will need the "Clean Side Tools" membership.

I'm a complete beginner. Can I still join?

Absolutely! We welcome makers of all skill levels. Our community members are happy to help beginners get started with basic techniques and equipment operation.

Do I need to bring my own supplies?

Please bring your own materials (fabric, yarn, thread, etc.) for your projects. We have equipment and tools, but members supply their own consumable materials.

What types of fiber arts are practiced at the meetup?

Our community includes experts in knitting, loom knitting, crochet, embroidery, cross stitch, felting, sewing, rug tufting, and more. If it involves fiber or textiles, someone in our group probably does it!

Can I get training on the sewing machines?

Yes! We offer Sewing Machines 101 workshops and experienced members are available to help you learn the equipment. Training ensures you can use the machines safely and effectively.

What if I can't make it every Saturday?

No problem! Come when you can. While the meetup is every Saturday at 4pm, you're welcome to use the fiber arts room anytime during open hours as a member.
