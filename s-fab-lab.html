Mission

The Kzoo Makers Fabrication Lab exists to provide accessible digital fabrication tools and rapid prototyping capabilities to the Kalamazoo community. We believe that everyone should have access to the tools and knowledge needed to turn ideas into reality.

Our goal is to create a collaborative environment where makers, students, entrepreneurs, and hobbyists can access professional-grade fabrication equipment without the high cost of individual ownership.

Fabrication Lab Overview

The Fabrication Lab at Kalamazoo Makerspace is a complete digital fabrication and rapid prototyping facility. We offer access to professional-grade equipment that would be cost-prohibitive for most individuals to own, along with the community support to help you use these tools effectively.

Our fabrication lab includes:

  • 3D Printing Lab with multiple FDM printers
  • Industrial Laser Cutting & Engraving
  • CNC Machining capabilities
  • Electronics Prototyping stations
  • Digital Design workstations
  • Post-processing and finishing areas
  • Rapid prototyping tools and materials
  • 3D scanning equipment
  • Vinyl cutting for signage and decals
  • Small-scale CNC routing
  • Electronics testing and debugging
  • Collaborative project spaces
  • Material testing and experimentation
  • Project documentation stations
  • Tool calibration and maintenance
  • Safety equipment and training
  • Community knowledge sharing
  • Interdisciplinary project support

Members regularly collaborate on projects, share techniques for optimizing fabrication workflows, and help each other troubleshoot complex builds. Whether you're working with digital designs, physical prototypes, or mixed-media projects, our fabrication lab provides the tools and community support to bring your ideas to life.
