# KzooMakers Flier Design Plan
**Multi-Purpose Promotional Flier | 8.5" x 11" Letter Size**

---

## Executive Summary

This plan outlines three distinct layout concepts for a versatile KzooMakers promotional flier designed for general awareness, tour promotion, and membership recruitment across multiple distribution channels (community boards, local businesses, coffee shops, and educational institutions).

---

## 1. Target Audience Analysis

### Primary Audiences:
- **Makers & Hobbyists**: DIY enthusiasts, crafters, tinkerers (ages 25-55)
- **Students**: College students from WMU and Kalamazoo College (ages 18-25)
- **Entrepreneurs**: Small business owners needing prototyping space (ages 30-50)
- **Professionals**: Engineers, designers seeking creative outlet (ages 28-45)
- **Retirees**: Experienced craftspeople looking for community (ages 55+)

### Distribution Contexts:
- Community bulletin boards (libraries, community centers)
- Local coffee shops and cafes
- University campus boards
- Local businesses and maker-friendly stores
- Community events and fairs

---

## 2. Key Messaging Hierarchy

### Primary Message (Hero):
**"Where Creativity Meets Craftsmanship"**
- Emphasizes the collaborative, hands-on nature

## 4. Visual Design Elements & Branding

### Color Palette:
Based on your website branding:

**Primary Colors:**
- **Gold/Yellow**: `#f9b234` (accent, highlights, icons)
- **Dark Gray/Charcoal**: `#333333` (primary text)
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` (background, breathing room)

**Secondary Colors:**
- **Light Gray**: `#f8f9fa` (section backgrounds)
- **Medium Gray**: `#6c757d` (secondary text)
- **Black**: `#000000` (headers, emphasis)

**Accent Colors:**
- **Red**: `#ff6b6b` (call-to-action elements)
- **Blue**: `#007bff` (links, trust elements)

### Typography Recommendations:

**Headers:**
- Font: Bold, sans-serif (Arial Black, Helvetica Bold, or Montserrat Bold)
- Size: 24-36pt for main header
- Style: All caps for impact, or Title Case for readability

**Body Text:**
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica, or Open Sans)
- Size: 10-12pt for body, 14-16pt for subheaders
- Line spacing: 1.3-1.5 for readability

**Emphasis:**
- Bold for key terms
- Gold color for pricing and benefits
- Icons instead of bullet points where possible

### Logo Usage:
- Use [`images/logo.png`](images/logo.png) for light backgrounds
- Use [`images/logo-white.png`](images/logo-white.png) for dark backgrounds
- Maintain clear space around logo (minimum 0.5" margins)
- Logo should be 2-3" wide for visibility

### Photography Guidelines:
Available images from your website:
- Workshop action shots: [`images/company/gallery-*.jpg`](images/company/)
- Equipment close-ups: [`images/wood-shop/`](images/wood-shop/), [`images/metal-shop/`](images/metal-shop/)
- Laser cutter: [`images/photoreel/laser/trotec-speedy500.webp`](images/photoreel/laser/trotec-speedy500.webp)

**Photo Selection Criteria:**
- Show people actively making/collaborating
- Display variety of equipment
- Capture the community atmosphere
- Ensure good lighting and focus
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds

### Icon System:
Use consistent icon style throughout:
- Simple line icons or solid filled icons
- Gold color (`#f9b234`) for visual consistency
- Size: 40-60px for section headers
- Available clipart: [`images/clipart/`](images/clipart/)

## 6. Technical Specifications

### Print Specifications:

**Document Setup:**
- Size: 8.5" x 11" (Letter)
- Orientation: Portrait
- Bleed: 0.125" (if using full-bleed design)
- Safe zone: 0.25" margin from edges
- Resolution: 300 DPI minimum

**Color Mode:**
- **For Professional Printing**: CMYK color mode
- **For Office/Home Printing**: RGB color mode is acceptable

**File Formats:**
- **Design Source**: Adobe Illustrator (.ai), InDesign (.indd), or Canva
- **Print-Ready**: PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4 (with embedded fonts)
- **Digital Distribution**: High-res PDF or PNG (300 DPI)

### Typography Specifications:

**Recommended Font Sizes:**
- Main Headline: 28-36pt, Bold
- Subheadline: 18-24pt, Semi-bold
- Section Headers: 14-18pt, Bold
- Body Text: 10-12pt, Regular
- Fine Print: 8-9pt, Regular
- Contact Info: 11-13pt, Regular or Semi-bold

**Font Embedding:**
- Always embed or outline fonts in final PDF
- Use web-safe fonts for easy editing: Arial, Helvetica, Georgia

### Image Specifications:

**Photo Requirements:**
- Resolution: 300 DPI at final print size
- Format: JPEG (high quality) or PNG
- Color profile: sRGB for digital, CMYK for print
- Compression: Minimal (quality 90-100%)

**Logo Requirements:**
- Vector format preferred (.svg, .ai, .eps)
- Minimum size: 2" wide for readability
- Clear space: 0.25" around logo

### QR Code Specifications:

**Technical Requirements:**
- Minimum size: 1" x 1" (0.75" for high-quality prints)
- Error correction: Level M or H (medium to high)
- Quiet zone: 4 modules (white space around code)
- Test on multiple devices before printing

**QR Code Content:**
- Primary: Tour signup form URL
- Alternative: Main website (kzoomakers.org)
- Use URL shortener for cleaner QR code

## 7. Printing Recommendations

### Budget-Friendly Options:

**Option 1: In-House Printing**
- Equipment: Office laser printer or inkjet
- Paper: 24-32 lb bright white paper or cardstock
- Cost: ~$0.10-0.25 per flier
- Best for: Small batches (10-50 fliers)

**Option 2: Local Print Shop**
- Vendors: FedEx Office, Staples, local print shops
- Paper: 80-100 lb gloss or matte cardstock
- Cost: ~$0.50-1.50 per flier (bulk discounts available)
- Best for: Medium batches (50-500 fliers)

**Option 3: Online Print Services**
- Vendors: Vistaprint, GotPrint, PrintPlace
- Paper: Various options, 100 lb gloss text recommended
- Cost: ~$0.20-0.60 per flier (bulk orders)
- Best for: Large batches (500+ fliers)

### Paper Recommendations:

**Standard Distribution:**
- 28-32 lb bright white paper
- Matte finish for easy writing/notes
- Cost-effective for high volume

**Premium Distribution:**
- 80-100 lb cardstock
- Gloss or matte finish
- More durable, professional appearance
- Better for long-term bulletin board display

**Eco-Friendly Option:**
- Recycled paper (30-50% post-consumer)
- Uncoated or matte finish
- Aligns with maker/sustainability values

## 13. Accessibility Considerations

### Visual Accessibility:
- **Font Size**: Minimum 10pt for body text, 14pt for important info
- **Contrast**: Ensure 4.5:1 contrast ratio (text to background)
- **Color**: Don't rely solely on color to convey information
- **Spacing**: Adequate line spacing (1.3-1.5) for readability

### Inclusive Language:
- "All skill levels welcome"
- "Beginner-friendly training provided"
- "Accessible workspace" (if applicable)
- Gender-neutral language throughout

### QR Code Alternatives:
- Include website URL in large, readable text
- Provide phone number for those without smartphones
- Consider adding tear-off tabs with contact info
